AI-Driven Demand Management Assistant

A conversational AI layer that connects A-ah!, Microsoft Teams, SharePoint, and Jira into a single intelligent interface — making demand knowledge instantly accessible to every stakeholder, at any time.

Prepared: June 2025
Alberto Pavani
Status: Prototype ready — Phase 1 approval requested
The problem we are solving
Today — fragmented
Demand status lives in A-ah!, decisions in Teams, documents in SharePoint, progress in Jira — no single view exists
Every status question interrupts the demand management team, creating a communication bottleneck
Meeting decisions disappear into chat history within days of each session
Stakeholders wait for responses that depend on individual availability and working hours
With the AI Assistant
One conversational interface surfaces information from all four systems in a single cited answer
Repetitive status queries answered instantly — demand managers focus on decisions, not information relay
Copilot meeting minutes indexed and searchable within minutes of every meeting ending
Available 24/7 to all authorised stakeholders — no dependency on individual availability

Three asks for this meeting
1

Confirm A-ah! API availability

This is the single most critical dependency. Can someone confirm whether A-ah! exposes a REST API and identify the right vendor contact? Without this, Phase 1 cannot begin. All other milestones depend on it.

2

Approve Phase 1 scoping

Authorise a formal scoping exercise (2–3 weeks) to validate the architecture, confirm API access for all four source systems, and produce a detailed project plan and cost estimate before committing to full development.

3

Designate an executive sponsor

The initiative requires a named executive sponsor to facilitate cross-functional decisions — particularly around data access, IT security approval, and the GDPR/DPIA assessment required before indexing internal documents.
