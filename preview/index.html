The challenge
Demand knowledge is everywhere.
Finding it shouldn't be.

Today, answering a simple question about a demand requires navigating four different systems and often interrupting the people who manage them.

Today — fragmented
Status lives in A-ah!, decisions in Teams, documents in SharePoint, progress in Jira — no single view
Every status question interrupts the demand management team, creating a communication bottleneck
Meeting decisions and action items disappear into chat history within days
Stakeholders wait for responses that depend on individual availability and working hours
With the AI Assistant
One conversational interface surfaces information from all four systems in a single cited answer
Repetitive status queries answered instantly — demand managers focus on decisions, not information relay
Copilot meeting minutes are indexed and searchable within minutes of every meeting ending
Available 24/7 to all authorized stakeholders — no dependency on individual availability

Architecture
Six layers. Zero disruption
to existing tools.

The assistant sits on top of what you already use. Nothing changes in A-ah!, Teams, SharePoint, or Jira — the AI adds a unified intelligence layer above them.

Consumers
Executives, demand managers, developers, business stakeholders — all with role-appropriate access
AI Agent Interface
Conversational entry point — natural language in, cited answers out, via Teams bot or web
Intelligence Core
RAG engine, intent router, context builder, response synthesizer — grounded in real data
Connector Layer
Read-only adapters for A-ah!, Teams/Copilot, SharePoint, and Jira — source systems unchanged
Source Systems
A-ah! · Microsoft Teams + Copilot · SharePoint · Jira — your existing tools, untouched
Knowledge Store
Vector DB, metadata cache, indexing pipeline, and role-based access control
