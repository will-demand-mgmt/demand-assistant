AI-Driven Demand Management Assistant

A conversational AI layer designed to centralize enterprise demand information, simplify data access, and improve operational decision-making across existing platforms.

Overview

Today, demand management information is distributed across multiple enterprise platforms such as Aha!, Microsoft Teams, SharePoint, and Jira. Retrieving and correlating data across these systems often requires manual effort, leading to fragmented visibility, slower response times, and inconsistent understanding of priorities and delivery status.

The proposed solution introduces a conversational AI layer capable of connecting these systems into a unified experience without replacing the current ecosystem.

Users can interact with the platform through natural language via chat, Microsoft Teams bots, or a web interface, receiving contextualized responses aggregated from multiple enterprise systems.

Architecture Approach

The solution is designed as an orchestration and intelligence layer positioned above existing enterprise tools.

Aha! remains the system of record for demands, Jira continues handling operational tracking, SharePoint manages documentation, and Microsoft Teams remains the collaboration layer.

Consumers

  • Executives
  • Demand Managers
  • Developers & PMs
  • Business Stakeholders

AI Assistant Layer

Conversational interface responsible for understanding intent, context, and orchestrating information retrieval.

Enterprise Connectors

  • Aha!
  • Microsoft Teams
  • SharePoint
  • Jira

Intelligence Core

The intelligence layer is composed of four core components:

  • RAG Engine for retrieval-augmented generation
  • Intent Router for connector orchestration
  • Context Builder for contextual enrichment
  • Response Synthesizer for consolidated answers with source citations
This architecture enables responses that are not only informative, but also contextualized, traceable, and role-aware.

Knowledge Layer

Supporting the assistant is a dedicated enterprise knowledge layer designed to improve retrieval quality and response performance.

Vector Database

Semantic indexing and retrieval of enterprise knowledge.

Metadata Cache

Demand IDs, ownership, status, and contextual metadata.

Indexing Pipeline

Scheduled and event-triggered synchronization processes.

Access Control Layer

Role-based visibility and enterprise governance.

Business Value

  • Reduce time spent retrieving distributed information
  • Improve visibility across demands and delivery status
  • Strengthen alignment between business and delivery teams
  • Create a unified access point for operational knowledge
  • Support faster and more context-aware decision-making

Roadmap & Evolution

The current proposal is defined as Phase 1 – Read-only, primarily focused on intelligent search and information aggregation.

Future phases may evolve toward:

  • Workflow orchestration
  • Advanced automation
  • Bidirectional enterprise updates
  • AI-assisted operational actions
  • Expanded analytics and governance capabilities
The objective is not to introduce another standalone tool, but to create an intelligent cross-platform layer that enhances the value of the systems already adopted within the organization.
